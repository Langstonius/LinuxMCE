Want to make some easy money?

Just pick a user name for yourself here.  Then tell your friends to check out Fiire and to provide your username as the "Referred by" when they sign up.  If one of your friends makes a purchase, you get a 10% commission.  If any of the people your friends refer make a purchase, then your friend gets the 10% commission, and you get a 4% commission.  And if one of them refers someone who makes a purchase, the direct referrer gets the 10% commission, your friend gets the 4% commission, and you still get a 1% commission.

So it works up to 3 levels down.  If you refer 10 people, each of them refers 10 people, and each of them refers another 10 people, that means you will have indirectly referred 1,000 people, and if any one of them makes a purchase, you get a commission.

You don’t even need to provide any personal information to get going.  Just pick a user name, tell your friends to check out Fiire, and check back periodically to see how many people signed up based on your initial referrals and how much commission you’ve earned.  Then give us a paypal account, e-gold account, or bank account to transfer your money to.
