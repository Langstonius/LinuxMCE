Fiire is the most advanced solution for home automation and media control available on the shelves right now, which does everything that the $100,000+ solutions from Crestron, Control 4, AMX, Kaleidescape, LifeWare and Escient do.
See next a short description of Fiire.

Enjoy media
From now on you'll find media more existing!
With Fiire you can watch TV (cable box or satellite) and record your favorite shows, watch a DVD or listen a CD; copy a DVD or CD to your Fiire Engine; watch or listen stored media; play the same media in multiple rooms simultaneously; catalog media with attributes; search media for attributes; make your media follow you in the house, or make announcements.

Throw all remotes
You don't need more than one remote from now on. Fiire is controlling everything and allows you to use a single remote for turning all TV's on, stream media to whole house, turn the Jacuzzi on when heading to your house from work. You can do all these, and more, by just using the mobile phone, the gyro remote, any IR remote that you're comfortable with, or a web tablet. So, you can throw away all your remotes and use only one device, the one that you love most.

Control regular devices
You can control light switches, brightness sensors, any IR climate devices, arm or disarm security panels; surveillance cameras, regular A/V devices with IR or RS232, regular phones or VOIP phones, etc.

What you can do with Fiire

  • You can make things happen at sunset or sunrise;
  • Make things happen when entering/leaving the room;
  • Make the lights, climate, media settings follow you;
  • Control everything from a floor plan;
  • You can browse a web page or switch to computing applications like document editing or games;
  • You can set Fiire to turn all your lights or media off when you go to sleep;
  • Turn your TV off when you don't use it;
  • Do video conferencing; listen to any offline messages;
  • Get notified with any security alerts; watch the screenshots associated with alerts;
  • Create your own skin for Fiire.

Fiire doesn't need special conditions for storage, in fact you can mount it directly to the back of your TV and it's quiet since it doesn't use fans and has no moving parts.
