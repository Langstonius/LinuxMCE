To eliminate the clutter of cables and a/v gear, get 1 FiireEngine for the home. Gather up all the a/v sources, such as TiVo's, VCR's, cable boxes, etc., that you have scattered throughout your home, and put them all in one place, connected to the FiireEngine. Then hide a FiireStation behind each TV. The FiireChief is then the only remote you'll need, and it will work the same in each room in the home, regardless of the brand of your TV. At each FiireStation you will have an HD PVR and media player, and the FiireStation will present the list of a/v devices you connected to the FiireEngine. When you choose a device, the audio & video are forwarded from the FiireEngine to the FiireStation, and whatever buttons you press on the FiireChief remote are relayed back to the FiireEngine, which sends them to your source device as i/r codes. The end result is that in each room you have only your flat panel TV on the wall, with no ugly cables or set top boxes, and you have 1 remote that controls everything and works in every room.
