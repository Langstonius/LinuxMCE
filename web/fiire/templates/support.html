Email:
Send us an email at support@fiire.com and someone will reply shortly.

Trouble tickets:
You can see the status of your trouble ticket by following the link http://fiire.com/tickets/
The tickets are added by qualified personnel but can be viewed anytime if you provide the trouble id.

On-line support
Talk with someone about your issues, or just call (877) on-fiire.
Someone is always on-line to take your call.
A person will add a new trouble ticket when talking with you and later you can view it in our system.

Product literature
The user manual is supplied with the product but it can be viewed on-line also. It can be found at this link http://fiire.com/User_manual/
You can also check: Brochures, Specs sheet and Overviews.

On-line help
