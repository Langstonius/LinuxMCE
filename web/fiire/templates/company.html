The manufacturing, order fulfillment, and warranty repairs for Fiire products are provided by Polywell Computers, of San Francisco.  Polywell is a highly respected manufacturer that has been delivering millions of OEM and retail direct systems for over 20 years, and whose products are regarded as high quality and have received numerous awards, such as PC World's Best Buy, PC Magazine-Editor's Choice, and Infoworld-Buyers' Assurance Seals.

Fiire provides the tech support, customer call center, and web site promotion.  Fiire is unique in many ways.  First, most tech companies outsource the support to low cost call centers with employees that have only minimal training.  Fiire instead has hired actual LinuxMCE gurus and enthusiasts from all over the world who typically work from home and know LinuxMCE inside out.  So when you call Fiire support, you’re reaching someone who knows what they’re talking about.  And we closely monitor the feedback callers leave to be sure each support rep provides only the best support.  Also, instead of spending money on marketing, Fiire gives back to its customers as a referral fee the 15% that would typically be used to market a product.  Fiire also aggressively supports the open source movement by providing free hardware and cash bonuses for developers who make contributions.
