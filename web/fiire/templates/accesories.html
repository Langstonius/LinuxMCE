CISCO 7970 phone is plug-in-play device and Fiire adds it automatically when it'll be plugged in the network. You can use it to make calls and control your home as well.



Network cameras or IP cameras are digital cameras that have a built-in web server. This allows the camera to be accessed by any computer on the same network using a web browser. Video is streamed to the web browser from the camera without additional equipment. In a Fiire system, the IP cameras are very easy to setup because they are plug-in-play. They can be wired or wireless.




Hauppauge PVR USB2
You can watch and record TV, pause TV with instant replay, record to disk your home videos, or listen to FM radio. It’s also plug-in-play, so it installs very easy with Fiire.




HDHomeRun is a Networked Digital TV Tuner. You can watch over-the-air digital TV from all computers in your home. It’s a dual tuner - Record/watch multiple channels at once.
