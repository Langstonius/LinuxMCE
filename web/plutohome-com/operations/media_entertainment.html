Media + Entertainment Feature Summary

• CD/DVD/DVD-RW/DVD+RW reader and recorder
• PVR with dual inputs (satellite and cable, for example)
• Video outputs: DVI, VGA, S-video, Composite, Component (using separate adapter)
• Audio outputs: digital spdif, and analog
• Dual-standard: NTSC or PAL
• Video resolutions: standard, progressive, 720p, 1080i, 1080p, as well as various custom resolutions for high-res monitors
• Firewire for capturing from digital camcorders
• Reader for digital film
• Combined on-screen program guide with all tv sources
• Central storage of all media with up to 10TB capacity
• Plays all standard media formats (.mp3, .ogg, .mpg, etc.)
• Compatible with 200 disc CD/DVD jukebox for rapid storage of large libraries
